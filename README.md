## What is.. ?

### HarvardX CS50
HarvardX CS50, also known as "CS50's Introduction to Computer Science," is an online course offered by Harvard University through the edX platform. It is a renowned introductory computer science course that covers fundamental topics in computer science and programming. With engaging lectures, challenging problem sets, and a supportive online community, this course is suitable for both beginners and more experienced learners. HarvardX CS50 provides a comprehensive foundation in computer science, including concepts like algorithms, data structures, software development, and web programming. It's an excellent choice for anyone looking to start their journey into the world of computer science.

### freeCodeCamp
freeCodeCamp is a nonprofit organization and online learning platform that offers a comprehensive and free coding curriculum. With a focus on web development and programming, it provides interactive coding challenges, projects, and certifications. Learners can acquire coding skills, work on real-world projects, and earn certifications in various programming disciplines, making freeCodeCamp an excellent resource for aspiring developers. It's known for its supportive community and commitment to providing accessible, quality coding education to people around the world.

### Codewars
Codewars is an online platform designed for programmers to enhance their coding skills through interactive challenges known as "kata." Users can choose from a variety of programming languages and difficulty levels to solve these coding problems. Codewars gamifies the learning process, allowing members to earn points and ranks as they complete challenges and engage with a vibrant coding community. It's a dynamic and engaging platform that promotes skill development and collaborative learning for developers of all levels.
